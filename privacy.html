2. Information We Collect

Your data is used only to provide app functionality: calculating pay, storing shifts, and syncing your settings across devices.

All information is stored securely in Google Firebase infrastructure with authentication and encryption. Only you have access to your data. Connections are always made over HTTPS.

5. Sharing of Data

We do not sell, trade, or share your information with third parties. Firebase acts solely as a data processor under Google’s GDPR-compliant terms.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

Your information remains until you delete your account or request deletion. You may contact us to permanently erase your account data.

7. Legal Basis (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (EU), processing is based on your consent and legitimate interest to provide you the service. You can withdraw consent anytime.
