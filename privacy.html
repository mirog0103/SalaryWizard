Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

SalaryWizard does not collect personal data beyond what is necessary for app functionality. Authentication is handled by Firebase, and optional work data you input (e.g., shifts, earnings) is stored securely in your account only.

Information Collection

We collect data only for your own use within the app. No information is shared with third parties.

Data Security

All data is transmitted securely over HTTPS and stored in Google Firebase with user-based access control.
