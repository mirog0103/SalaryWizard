Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how SalaryWizard ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the SalaryWizard mobile application ("the App").

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: If you register or sign in, Firebase Authentication stores your email address for login purposes.
  • Shift and Salary Data: All work entries, shift times, and salary settings you enter are saved privately to your Firebase account.
  • Device Data: Firebase may automatically collect basic technical information (e.g., device type, OS version, app version) to ensure performance and reliability.

2. How We Use the Information

We use your data solely to:

  • Provide your personalized shift and salary calculations;
  • Sync your data securely across your devices;
  • Maintain and improve app functionality.

3. Data Storage and Security

All information is stored securely in Google Firebase (Firestore and Authentication). Data is transmitted using SSL/HTTPS. Each user’s data is isolated using Firebase security rules — only you can access your data.

4. Sharing of Data

We do not share, sell, or rent your personal information to any third parties. Data may be processed by Google Firebase as our trusted service provider.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data remains in your account until you delete it. You can request full data deletion by emailing us at salarywizard@gmail.com.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for users aged 16 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 16. If you believe a child has used the App, please contact us for removal.

7. Your Rights (GDPR / EU)

If you are located in the EU, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your data, and to withdraw consent at any time. Contact salarywizard@gmail.com to exercise your rights.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The latest version will always be available at this URL.

9. Contact Us
